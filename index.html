Shoe Inventory System Up to 6 phone photos Price always required Box or no box prompt

1. Capture photos

Use the phone camera or gallery. The app previews up to six images and uploads them to Supabase Storage when you save the pair.

2. Answer box or no box

If the pair has a box, the AI action is geared toward reading the label. If there is no box, you can type the details manually or ask AI to infer them from the shoe photos.

3. Confirm and save

Brand, description, size, and price are stored in Supabase along with the box status and photo URLs.
